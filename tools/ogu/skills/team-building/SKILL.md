# Team Building

## When to Use
- Scaling an engineering team during a growth phase
- Rebuilding team culture after organizational changes
- Improving team collaboration and psychological safety

## Workflow
1. Define team health metrics: eNPS, DORA metrics, cycle time, and meeting load
2. Establish team norms: communication channels, decision-making, code review SLAs
3. Run regular retrospectives to surface and address team friction
4. Build inclusion: ensure all voices are heard in technical discussions
5. Create learning culture: encourage knowledge sharing, postmortems without blame

## Quality Bar
- Team norms are documented and visible to all members
- Retrospective action items are tracked and followed up
- New team members are productive within 2 weeks of joining
- Team health reviewed quarterly with direct input from team members
