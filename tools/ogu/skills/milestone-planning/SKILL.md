# Milestone planning

## When to Use

Activate this skill when:
- Working on milestone planning tasks
- Reviewing or improving existing milestone planning implementations
- Troubleshooting issues related to milestone planning
- Setting up or configuring milestone planning from scratch

## Workflow

1. Gather context, goals, constraints, and stakeholder expectations
2. Align with key stakeholders on priorities and success criteria
3. Develop a clear plan, framework, or structure
4. Communicate decisions and expectations clearly to all parties
5. Execute with regular check-ins and adaptive adjustments
6. Track progress against goals and surface blockers early
7. Resolve impediments or escalate with context
8. Document decisions, rationale, and outcomes
9. Conduct retrospective and capture learnings for future cycles

## Quality Bar

- Stakeholders informed, aligned, and unblocked
- Decisions documented with rationale
- Progress tracked with clear visibility
- Learnings captured and shared
- Team health maintained
