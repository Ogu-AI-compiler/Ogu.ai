# Stakeholder Communication

## When to Use
- Presenting a technical plan to non-technical leadership
- Writing weekly or monthly status updates for a project
- Managing expectations during an incident or project delay

## Workflow
1. Lead with the business impact, not the technical details
2. Use the inverted pyramid: most important information first
3. Translate technical metrics into business terms (uptime → customer hours affected)
4. Proactively communicate risks and delays before they are asked about
5. End every update with clear next steps and owners

## Quality Bar
- Executive updates are under 1 page and answer: status, risks, decisions needed
- Technical jargon replaced with plain language or defined on first use
- Risk communication includes mitigation plan, not just the risk
- Updates are timely — stakeholders hear from you before they ask
