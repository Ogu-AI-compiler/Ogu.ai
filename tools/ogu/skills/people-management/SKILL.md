# People Management

## When to Use
- Running performance review cycles
- Addressing underperformance with a direct report
- Planning career development for a team member

## Workflow
1. Set clear expectations: define what good performance looks like at each level
2. Give real-time feedback — don't save all feedback for the review cycle
3. Use structured frameworks for reviews: impact, delivery, collaboration
4. Address underperformance early with a clear improvement plan (PIP if escalated)
5. Invest in growth: identify stretch opportunities aligned with career goals

## Quality Bar
- Every direct report has a clear understanding of their performance standing
- Feedback is documented and not just verbal
- Performance reviews are calibrated across managers to ensure fairness
- Development plans are reviewed quarterly, not just at annual review time
